All About Enhypen

Korean Pop Boy Group

ENHYPEN (엔하이픈) is the final 7 members of the survival show I-LAND under BE:LIFT Lab, a joint label created by HYBE and CJ E&M Entertainment (previously known as BigHit Entertainment). The group consists of Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, Jungwon, and Ni-ki. ENHYPEN is a mix of an en dash and a hyphen that connects different words to create new meaning, members of ENHYPEN will connect, discover each other, and grow together. ENHYPEN debuted on November 30th, 2020 with their mini-album “BORDER: DAY ONE” and title track “Given-Taken”.

Things To Know About Enhypen

1. They are last and final seven contestants on I-Land, a music survival show.
2. The hyphen symbol serves as the inspiration for their groud name (En-).
3. Some members of the group are from the US, Australia, Japan, South Korea, and the US.
4. ENGENE, the name of their fandom, has two distinct meanings.
5. All of the members are tall.
